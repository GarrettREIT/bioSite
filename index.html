About George

George worked for Southern Pacific Railroad for the majority of his life. Happily retired at age 70 he spends the majority of his time with close friends and family, traveling whenever possible and still actively in the music scene supporting his talented grandson Khaymonds drumming career. Southern Pacific awarded George many great opportunities including relocating to two different states. Click the About Tab to learn more!

George's Hobbies

George enjoys getting out to see live music, no matter the venue. Between his grandson being a talented drummer and having various other talented musician friends, George always knows where to find the good music in town. Traveling to 4 different countries, and all over the United States, George has some stories to tell. You can find him most days in his living room with a revolving door of friends and family coming in and out unexpected. Taking the open-door policy to a whole new level, stop on in for good people and great conversation. Click the Hobbies Tab to lean more!
